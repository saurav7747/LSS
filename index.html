Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence

Lakhotia Computer Centre (LCC) is a premier computer training institute located in Barauni, Begusarai, Bihar — committed to delivering industry-standard IT education to students of all backgrounds.

At LCC, we believe every student deserves access to quality technology education. Our structured programs, experienced faculty, and hands-on curriculum prepare students for a competitive digital world.

From basic computing to advanced professional diplomas, we offer a learning path tailored to your goals — whether you're starting fresh or upskilling for career growth.
